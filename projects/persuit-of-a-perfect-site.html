In this blog we will go through the history of how step by step this was made.
Last year Tony Davis became my mentor. He was/is a amzing guy had worked in militry then shifted to academics. Another thing about him was his personal website, it was beutiful and very well made. This was the ignition which I needed to start wanting to have one site for myself also.
